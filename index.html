Providing Landscape Consultation and Design Services for Over 40 Years.

We offer a wide range of landscape design services for front yards, back yards and entire properties, including gardens, patios, decks, retaining walls, driveways, walkways, water features, swimming pools, outdoor structures, built-in barbecues, outdoor kitchens, fencing, gates, and lighting.

Our Services

Tailored plans to bring your outdoor vision to life. Whether you know exactly what you want or need help figuring it out, we design balanced, detailed layouts just for you.

  • Design Consultation - On-site meeting to understand your goals, style, and budget.
  • Conceptual Design - Scaled concepts with key features like seating areas, gardens, or water elements.
  • Landscape Master Plan - Final design with full details: materials, plants, lighting, and more.

Meet Peter Reid

“Transforming outdoor spaces for over 40 years.”

Peter Reid, a native Haligonian, discovered his passion for landscape design as a teen. After co-founding MacKinnon Reid & Associates in Ottawa and completing over 1000 creative projects, he returned to Halifax to raise his family and launch Peter Reid Consulting. With over 40 years of experience, Peter brings a unique, interactive approach to landscape design in the Maritimes—transforming outdoor spaces to be more beautiful, functional, and valuable.
