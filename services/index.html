Our Services

We help homeowners throughout the Maritimes turn their outdoor dreams into reality. Whether you're starting from scratch or want to enhance an existing space, we design with creativity, purpose, and years of experience.

Design Consultation

We begin with a site visit and in-depth discussion to understand your needs, budget, and vision. Our goal is to create a space that reflects your lifestyle and enhances the beauty and value of your property.

Conceptual Design

From our initial meeting, we prepare a scaled design concept that outlines the key features of your spaceâ€”whether it's a patio, water feature, garden area, or outdoor kitchen. This stage helps visualize potential layouts and gather your feedback.

Landscape Master Plan

Once the concept is finalized, we prepare a comprehensive master plan with all the design details: materials, structures, lighting, plant selections, and installation notes. This plan can be used by you or your contractor to bring the design to life.
